A Testimony of Transformation

From Islam to the Love of Yesuah

Pastor Max Irhyam was raised in the Muslim faith — devout, sincere, and deeply committed to the religion of his upbringing. For much of his life, Islam was everything he knew. But God, in His sovereign love, had a different plan.

Through a profound and personal encounter with Jesus Christ — Yesuah — Pastor Max's life was turned completely upside down. What he found in Christ was not religion, but relationship. Not rules, but radical, unconditional love. Not ritual, but real healing for his heart, his mind, and his spirit.

That encounter did not just change his faith — it became his calling. Out of the depths of his own transformation, Yesuah's Love Heals Ministry was born: a ministry dedicated to bringing the same healing, freedom, and love to every person who walks through its doors.

Pastor Max's testimony stands as a living proof that no background, no religion, and no past is beyond the reach of God's love.

Pastor Quinn Irhyam Assistant Pastor

Standing faithfully alongside Pastor Max, Quinn Irhyam serves as Assistant Pastor of Yesuah's Love Heals. Her heart for people, her gift for pastoral care, and her deep love for God make her an irreplaceable pillar of this ministry. Together, Max and Quinn lead this community with a shared vision: that every person they encounter would know they are seen, loved, and healed by Yesuah.
